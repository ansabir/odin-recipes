Veg Biryani Recipe

In simple words Biryani is a rice and currry casserole. The curry can be meat based or vege table based.

Similar to some layered and baked pasta dishes, the curry is layered with semi-cooked rice later to be baked or dum cooked until the rice is completetly done and curry or gravvy has been absorbed by rice.

Ususally the meat is marinated and cooked later for the curry, but for vegetables marination is not done or is needed.

Biryani is also spelled as Biryani and is popular not only in India but in South Asia as well.

What ingredients should I use ?

  • Water
  • Veggies
  • Dairy
  • Seasonings

  • raisins
